Research carried out by (Tunggal, 2025) states that cybersecurity is crucial because it protects all categories of data from theft and damage. This includes sensitive data, personally identifiable information , personal information, intellectual property, data, and governmental and industry information systems. Without a cybersecurity program, your organization cannot defend itself against data breach campaigns, which makes it an easy target for cybercriminals.Cybersecurity is the state or process of protecting and recovering computer systems, networks, devices, and programs from any type of cyber attack.
